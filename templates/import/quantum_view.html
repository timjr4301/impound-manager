Import UPS Quantum View

Auto-update delivery confirmations from your daily UPS export
How It Works
  1. In UPS Quantum View, download your daily shipment detail report as CSV.
  2. Upload the CSV here. The app looks for a Tracking Number column and a Delivery Date or Status column.
  3. Letters with matching tracking numbers that show DELIVERED status will have their delivery date automatically recorded.
  4. Letters already confirmed, or with unrecognized tracking numbers, are skipped and reported.
Upload CSV File
CSV format. Encoding UTF-8 or UTF-8-BOM preferred; Latin-1 also handled.
Expected columns (any of these work):
Tracking Number, Package/Sequence Number, TrackingNumber
Delivery Date, Actual Delivery Date, DeliveryDate
Status, Status Description, Activity Description
